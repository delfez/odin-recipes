Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A vanilla milkshake recipe that's very easy to make. Add chocolate, berries, peaches, or whatever you like. Perfect for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • whipped cream

Directions

  1. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth; pour into glasses, add whipped cream on top and serve.
